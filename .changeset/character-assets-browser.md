Added a **Character Assets** browser and added it to the Character apps. You can now explore everything your character owns as a location tree — expand a station to see what's parked there, then drill into a ship or container to view its fitting, cargo and contents. Each location, ship and stack shows its estimated ISK value, and a search box finds any item across all your assets and tells you where it is.
